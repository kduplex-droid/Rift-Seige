RIFT SIEGE

A Three.js sci-fi shooter prototype inspired by industrial arena FPS design.

  • WASD = move
  • Mouse = aim
  • Left click = fire
  • Shift = sprint
  • Ctrl = crouch
  • Space = jump
  • R = reload

No external assets required. Runs directly in browser.
